If you want to use Carbide in a **closed-source or otherwise AGPL-incompatible**
product, you can purchase a commercial license. It grants the same code under
terms that **do not** require you to open-source your application.

To enquire, contact **peter.aleksander@bizjak.tech**.

A commercial license typically covers:

- Use of Carbide in proprietary, closed-source applications.
- No copyleft / source-disclosure obligation for your own code.
- Optional support terms.
